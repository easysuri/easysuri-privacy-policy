Privacy Policy for EasySuri — Period & Qada Solat Tracker

Effective Date: September 6, 2025

EasySuri ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. The App focuses on menstrual cycle tracking and optional Qada Solat tracking features.

1. Information We Collect

  • User-Provided Information: Cycle dates, period type labels (e.g., Haid, Istihadah, Nifas), notes, and any entries you choose to save.
  • Technical Information: Device model, operating system version, app version, and anonymized usage metrics for performance and stability.
  • Support Communications: Email or contact details you provide when requesting support.

2. How We Use Information

  • To deliver the App's core functionality (tracking, reminders, predictions, and Qada Solat calculations).
  • To improve and personalize the user experience and enhance prediction accuracy.
  • To detect, diagnose, and fix issues via analytics and crash reporting.
  • To respond to your support requests and inquiries.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data may be stored locally on your device and, if you enable cloud features, securely in cloud services. We employ standard security practices to protect data, but no service can guarantee absolute security. Do not include sensitive personal identifiers in free-text notes if you are concerned about privacy.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as analytics and crash reporting to help maintain and improve the App. These services process limited technical or anonymous data. We do not sell or trade your personal health information.

5. User Choices & Rights

  • You can access, correct, or delete information you have entered in the App.
  • You may disable optional cloud sync or backups in the App settings (if available).
  • You may uninstall the App at any time to stop further collection of data on your device.

6. Children

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age (or under 16 where required by local law). We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When changes are material, we will provide notice within the App or by the contact details you provided, and update the Effective Date above.
